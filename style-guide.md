# Little Lemon Brand Sheet

## Brand
**Little Lemon**  
Mediterranean restaurant brand  
Tone: warm, fresh, friendly, modern

---


## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

| Color | Hex |
|-----|-----|
| Dark Green | `#495E57` |
| Lemon Yellow | `#F4CE14` |

### Secondary Colors

| Color | Hex |
|-----|-----|
| Light Green | `#EDEFEE` |
| Dark Gray | `#333333` |
| White | `#FFFFFF` |

**Color usage**
- Dark green for headers and structure
- Yellow for accents and emphasis
- Light green or white for backgrounds
- Dark gray for text

---

## Typography

### Font Strategy
System fonts only

**Text rules**
- Headlines: bold or semibold
- Body: regular weight
- No decorative fonts
- High readability

---


## Design Principles

- Simple and clean
- High contrast
- Accessible
- Mobile-first
- Consistent spacing
